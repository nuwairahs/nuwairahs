BREAKFASTS

ENGLISH BREAKFAST $5.95

The English Breakfast, also known as a Full English Breakfast, is a classic and hearty morning meal traditionally enjoyed in the United Kingdom. It consists of a variety of ingredients that make it a filling and satisfying dish, often served with tea or coffee.

BOILED EGGS & ASPARAGUS $5.95

boiled eggs & asparagus is a simple, healthy, and delicious dish that combines soft or hard-boiled eggs with tender asparagus spears. often served with seasonings like salt, pepper, and olive oil, it can also be enhanced with hollandaise sauce or grated cheese. this dish is rich in protein, vitamins, and antioxidants, making it a nutritious choice for breakfast or a light meal.

BANANA PORRIDGE $5.95

Banana porridge is a warm, creamy, and nutritious breakfast dish made by cooking oats, cornmeal, or mashed bananas with milk or water. It is naturally sweet, rich in fiber, and packed with essential vitamins and minerals. Often enjoyed in Caribbean and African cuisine, banana porridge can be enhanced with cinnamon, nutmeg, honey, or nuts for added flavor. It provides sustained energy, making it an excellent choice for a healthy and filling breakfast.

BURGERS

HAMBURGER $5.95

A hamburger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked ground meat patty, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. It is often served with toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise. Originating from Germany and popularized in the United States, the hamburger has become a global fast-food staple, with various variations including chicken, turkey, and plant-based patties. It is commonly enjoyed with fries and a soft drink.

CHEESEBURGER $5.95

A cheeseburger is a variation of a hamburger that includes a slice of melted cheese on top of the meat patty. The cheese is typically added while the patty is still hot, allowing it to melt slightly. Common types of cheese used include American, cheddar, Swiss, or pepper jack. Like a regular hamburger, a cheeseburger is served in a bun and can be topped with lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and condiments such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise. It is a widely popular fast-food item enjoyed worldwide.

DOUBLE DECKER $5.95

A Double Decker typically refers to a sandwich or burger with two layers of fillings separated by an additional slice of bread or bun. In the fast-food world, a Double Decker Burger features two beef patties stacked with layers of cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and sauce between three pieces of bread or buns. This style of burger is known for its extra size and satisfying flavors.

MOUSAKA $5.95

Moussaka is a traditional Mediterranean and Middle Eastern dish, most commonly associated with Greek cuisine. It is a rich and hearty layered casserole made with eggplant (aubergine), minced meat (often lamb or beef), tomatoes, and a creamy béchamel sauce.

SOUVLAKI $5.95

Souvlaki is a popular Greek dish consisting of small pieces of grilled meat, usually pork, chicken, or lamb, often served on a skewer. It is a staple of Greek street food and can be eaten directly from the skewer, wrapped in pita bread with toppings like tomatoes, onions, and tzatziki sauce, or served on a plate with rice, fries, and salad.

STIFADO $5.95

Stifado is a traditional Greek stew known for its rich, aromatic flavors. It is typically made with slow-cooked beef, rabbit, or lamb, along with onions, tomatoes, and a blend of warm spices such as cinnamon, cloves, and bay leaves.

Sirloin Steak is a popular and versatile cut of beef known for its balance of tenderness and rich flavor. It is taken from the upper middle part of the cow, just behind the ribs, making it a slightly leaner option compared to ribeye but still juicy and flavorful.
