APERITIFS

VERMOUTH $5.95

**Vermouth** is a fortified wine that is flavored with various botanicals, herbs, spices, and sometimes fruits. It is typically used as a base for cocktails but can also be enjoyed on its own as an aperitif. There are two primary types of vermouth: **dry vermouth** (which is more bitter and often used in classic cocktails like the Martini) and **sweet vermouth** (which is richer, with a sweeter profile, and used in cocktails like the Negroni or Manhattan). Vermouth has a long history, originating in Italy and France, and is made by infusing wine with various herbs and botanicals such as wormwood, chamomile, and cloves. It has a unique, complex flavor profile that can range from herbal and spicy to floral and fruity, making it a versatile ingredient in many cocktails.

RAKI $5.95

Raki is a traditional Turkish alcoholic beverage made from distilled grapes and flavored with aniseed. It is often referred to as "Lion’s Milk" due to its cloudy white appearance when mixed with water. Typically enjoyed as an aperitif, raki is sipped slowly and is commonly accompanied by meze, a variety of small appetizers. Raki holds cultural significance in Turkey and neighboring countries, symbolizing friendship and hospitality. It’s often enjoyed in social gatherings, where people toast with the phrase "Şerefe!" meaning "Cheers!"

AMONTILLADO $5.95

Amontillado is a type of sherry wine from Spain, specifically from the Jerez region. It is a fortified wine made from the Palomino grape. Amontillado starts as a fino sherry, which undergoes a biological aging process under a layer of yeast called "flor," but later undergoes oxidative aging once the flor dies, giving it a nutty, rich flavor. The result is a dry, amber-colored wine with flavors of almonds, hazelnuts, and sometimes dried fruit. Amontillado has a complex profile and is enjoyed as an aperitif or paired with various dishes like seafood, cheeses, and cured meats.

BEERS

SCHNEIDER WEISSE $5.95

Schneider Weisse is a renowned German brewery that specializes in crafting wheat beers (Hefeweizens). Founded in 1872 in Kelheim, Bavaria, it is one of the oldest and most respected wheat beer breweries in Germany. Known for its high-quality, traditional brewing methods, Schneider Weisse produces a variety of wheat beers, including both dark (Dunkelweizen) and light (Hefeweizen) styles. Their beers are rich, flavorful, and often feature notes of banana, clove, caramel, and bread. Popular offerings include the Schneider Weisse Original (TAP7) and Schneider Weisse Aventinus, a strong and malty Doppelbock.

FRUH KOLSCH $5.95

Früh Kölsch is a traditional German beer brewed in Cologne (Köln), Germany. Kölsch is a light, pale, and crisp ale, known for its unique brewing style that combines the characteristics of both lager and ale. Früh Kölsch is one of the most popular and well-regarded Kölsch beers. It has a subtle bitterness with a slightly fruity aroma, balanced by a smooth malt profile. Typically served in small, tall glasses called "Stangen," it is best enjoyed fresh and is often paired with light foods such as fish, sausages, and salads. Früh Kölsch has become a symbol of Cologne’s beer culture.

EISBOCK $5.95

Eisbock is a strong, malty German beer style that originated in the Franconia region. It is made by freezing a bock beer and removing the ice, concentrating the alcohol and flavors. The result is a smooth, full-bodied beer with rich malt sweetness, notes of caramel, dark fruit, and a warming alcohol finish. Eisbocks typically have an alcohol content between 9-12% ABV, making them stronger than regular bocks. This beer is often enjoyed as a sipping beer due to its complexity and high alcohol content. It pairs well with rich dishes like stews, roasted meats, and strong cheeses.

BRANDIES

HENNESSY $5.95

Hennessy is a renowned brand of Cognac, a type of brandy produced in the Cognac region of France. It is made from distilled white wine and aged in oak barrels, which imparts a rich and complex flavor. Hennessy is known for its smoothness, with flavor profiles that include notes of vanilla, oak, fruit, and spice. The brand is famous for producing a variety of Cognac expressions, including VS (Very Special), VSOP (Very Superior Old Pale), and XO (Extra Old), each with different aging periods and taste characteristics. Hennessy is often enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails.

REMY MARTIN $5.95

Rémy Martin is a prestigious brand of Cognac, known for producing high-quality brandy from grapes exclusively grown in the Grande Champagne and Petite Champagne regions of the Cognac AOC (Appellation d'Origine Contrôlée) in France. These regions are recognized for their exceptional terroir, which contributes to the distinctive, refined taste of Rémy Martin Cognac.

METAXAS $5.95

Metaxa is a renowned Greek brand of aged spirits, often referred to as a "Greek brandy." It is produced by blending wine brandy with muscat wines from the Aegean islands and various botanicals, including herbs and spices, which give it a unique, aromatic flavor profile.

COCKTAILS

CUBA LIBRE $5.95

Cuba Libre is a classic cocktail made with three simple ingredients: rum, cola, and lime juice. The drink is traditionally made with white rum, but dark rum can also be used for a slightly richer flavor. The addition of fresh lime juice gives the cocktail its signature tangy taste, balancing the sweetness of the cola and the smoothness of the rum.

PINA COLADA $5.95

Piña Colada is a tropical cocktail that combines the refreshing flavors of pineapple, coconut, and rum. The drink is traditionally made by blending rum, coconut cream (or coconut milk), and pineapple juice with ice, resulting in a creamy, smooth, and sweet beverage. It is often garnished with a cherry or pineapple slice for a festive touch.

BLACK RUSSIAN $5.95

The Black Russian is a simple yet flavorful cocktail made with vodka and coffee liqueur (such as Kahlúa). It’s a straightforward and bold drink, with the smoothness of vodka complemented by the sweet, rich flavor of coffee liqueur.

COLD BEVERAGES

SMOOTHIE $5.95

A smoothie is a blended drink typically made with fruits, vegetables, yogurt, milk, or juice, and often combined with other ingredients like nuts, seeds, or protein powders. Smoothies are known for being nutritious, customizable, and refreshing, offering a convenient way to consume vitamins, fiber, and other healthy nutrients.

ICED TEA $5.95

Iced tea is a chilled beverage made by brewing tea (black, green, or herbal) and then cooling it down with ice. It’s a refreshing and versatile drink, often sweetened or flavored with fruits, herbs, or spices to suit personal tastes. Iced tea is a popular choice during hot weather due to its crisp, refreshing nature.

SOFT DRINK $5.95

A soft drink is a non-alcoholic beverage, typically carbonated and sweetened, that is often flavored with various ingredients such as fruit juices, herbs, or artificial flavorings. These drinks are enjoyed worldwide and come in a wide range of flavors, colors, and styles.

Coffee is a beloved beverage enjoyed worldwide, brewed from roasted beans. It’s loved for its rich flavor, caffeine boost, and versatility—whether hot, iced, black, or with milk and flavorings.

TEA $5.95

Tea is a soothing beverage made by steeping leaves in hot water. It’s enjoyed in various forms like black, green, herbal, or chai, offering a range of flavors and health benefits.

HOT CHOCOLATE $5.95

Hot chocolate is a warm, comforting drink made with melted chocolate, milk, and sugar. It's perfect for cozy moments, often topped with whipped cream or marshmallows, offering a rich, sweet indulgence.

LIQUEURS

TIA MARIA $5.95

Tia Maria is a dark coffee liqueur made with Jamaican rum, coffee beans, vanilla, and sugar. It has a rich, sweet flavor and is often enjoyed on its own, in cocktails, or over ice.

GALLIANO $5.95

Galliano is an Italian herbal liqueur known for its distinctive yellow color and sweet, aromatic flavor. Made with a blend of herbs, spices, and vanilla, it's often used in cocktails like the Harvey Wallbanger.

COINTREAU $5.95

Cointreau is a French triple sec liqueur made from sweet and bitter orange peels. It has a crisp, citrusy flavor and is commonly used in cocktails like the Margarita and Cosmopolitan.

MILKSHAKES

CHOCOLATE MILKSHAKE $5.95

A chocolate milkshake is a creamy, sweet drink made by blending chocolate ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup. It's a delicious treat, often topped with whipped cream and a cherry for extra indulgence.

BANANA MILKSHAKE $5.95

A banana milkshake is a creamy, sweet beverage made by blending ripe bananas, milk, and ice cream. It’s a refreshing, naturally flavored drink that can be enjoyed as a snack or dessert.

VANILLA MILKSHAKE $5.95

A vanilla milkshake is a smooth, creamy drink made by blending vanilla ice cream, milk, and a touch of vanilla extract. It's a classic, sweet treat that's perfect on its own or with toppings.

SHOOTERS

B-52 $5.95

Shooters are small, often layered alcoholic drinks typically served in shot glasses. They are consumed quickly in a single gulp and can be made with a variety of spirits, liqueurs, and mixers. Popular examples include the B-52, Jägerbomb, and Lemon Drop.

TNT $5.95

TNT is a popular shooter cocktail made with tequila and red bull, often served in a shot glass. The combination of the energy drink and the kick of tequila creates an intense, energizing drink.

A-BOMB $5.95

An A-Bomb is a popular cocktail shooter that typically consists of a shot of Amaretto liqueur dropped into a glass of energy drink, often Red Bull. The combination gives a sweet and energizing kick, with a strong flavor and buzz.

SPIRITS

SMIRNOFF VODKA $5.95

Smirnoff Vodka is a well-known, globally recognized brand of vodka, originally created in Russia. It's made from grains and distilled multiple times for smoothness. It's versatile, commonly used in cocktails like the Moscow Mule, Martini, or enjoyed straight or on the rocks.

CASA NOBLE TEQUILA $5.95

Casa Noble Tequila is a premium tequila brand from Mexico, known for its high-quality, 100% blue agave spirit. It offers a range of expressions, including Blanco, Reposado, and Añejo, each carefully crafted for smoothness and rich flavor. Casa Noble is recognized for its balanced, smooth, and complex taste, often enjoyed neat or in cocktails.

BACARDI RUM $5.95

Bacardi Rum is a well-known brand of rum originating from Cuba. It’s made from sugarcane and is available in various varieties, including white, gold, and dark rum. Bacardi is widely used in cocktails like the Mojito, Daiquiri, and Piña Colada, known for its smooth taste and versatility.

WHISKEYS

JAMESON $5.95

Jameson is a popular Irish whiskey known for its smooth, triple-distilled character. Made from a blend of malted and unmalted barley, it's renowned for its rich, slightly sweet flavor with hints of vanilla, oak, and spices. It’s often enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails like the Jameson & Ginger.

JOHNNIE WALKER $5.95

Johnnie Walker is a renowned Scotch whisky brand, famous for its wide range of blends. Known for its distinctive square bottles and iconic labels (Red, Black, Green, Gold, and Blue), it offers a smooth, rich flavor profile. The whisky is enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in classic cocktails like the Old Fashioned.

JACK DANIELS $5.95

Jack Daniel's is a famous American whiskey brand from Lynchburg, Tennessee. Known for its smooth, sweet flavor, it’s made from a blend of corn, rye, and malted barley, and filtered through charcoal before aging. Popular varieties include Old No. 7, Tennessee Honey, and Single Barrel. It's commonly enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails like the Lynchburg Lemonade.

WINES

Meyer is a brand of premium liqueur, often known for its Meyer Lemon flavor. It is made from a unique variety of lemons, which are sweeter and less acidic than regular lemons. Meyer Lemon liqueurs, like **Meyer’s Lemon Liqueur**, are often used in cocktails for a refreshing, citrusy twist. If you meant something else by "Meyer," feel free to clarify!

ALTESINO $5.95

Altesino is a prestigious Italian winery located in Tuscany, known for producing high-quality wines, particularly Brunello di Montalcino. The winery focuses on crafting wines with a strong sense of place, using traditional winemaking techniques combined with modern practices. Altesino's wines are renowned for their depth, complexity, and aging potential.

PAZO DE SENORANS $5.95

Pazo de Señorans is a renowned winery located in the Rías Baixas DO (Denomination of Origin) in Galicia, Spain. Specializing in Albariño, a white grape variety native to the region, Pazo de Señorans produces wines known for their crisp acidity, fresh citrus flavors, and mineral character. Their Albariño wines are highly regarded for their elegance and complexity.
